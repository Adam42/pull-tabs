About

Pulltabs was created to help pull tabs out of your current browser window and perform actions on them. Whether that's saving to your desktop, sharing to a social network or bookmarking locally or with a remote service like Pocket.

There are two views in Pulltabs, a simple view and and advanced view. You can use either or both at the same time. Simply check off which views you'd like chosen in the Options menu. The simple view sends all your tabs to a single destination or performs a single action on each tab. The advanced view is more granular and allows you to choose an action/destination per each tab, including deactivating particular tabs and having Pulltabs ignore them.
